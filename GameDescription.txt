CSC 2232 Spring 2015
FSC Adeventure Game
Theme of the game: soccer ball hunt
Challenges for the player: Clues
	The player will have to meet with different staff members, 
	faculty members, etc to get clues to, in the end, find the soccer 
	ball.
The ultimate goal of the game:  The players earns points going to the 
	right places and getting the right objects.
	The player need to find and bring back the soccer ball 
	to the Barnett Field on time for the national championship 
	final game that is happening on campus.
